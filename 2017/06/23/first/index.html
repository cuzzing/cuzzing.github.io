Compulsory voting

发表于 2017-06-23 更新于 2020-03-23

exercise my right to vote

constituency
the people who live in and vote in a particular area 选区；选区的全体选民

engender
=give rise to 造成，引起

the changes in society engendered by the war
⇒engender sth in sb
relationships that engender trust in children

slapdash
adj. do something quickly and carelessly

conscription

get it over and done with

pick (a political party) out of a hat
